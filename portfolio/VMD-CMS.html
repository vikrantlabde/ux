Portfolio: TheVisualMD - CMS

TheVisualMD website and application is a platform which brings together rich health information, wellness trackers, PHR tools and communities to offer a holistic approach towards wellbeing. To manage this vast content, the client needed a customised content management system.

We started with creating an interactive wireframe using Axure. Thereafter, a few iterations later, we designed a truly simpler way for content creators and admins to manage their workflow easily.
